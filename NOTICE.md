## ExCrap

The Change Risk Anti-Patterns (CRAP) scoring, cyclomatic-complexity analysis,
and coverage-ingestion logic used by
`OeditusCredo.Check.Refactoring.ChangeRiskAntiPatterns` were ported and adapted
from ExCrap (https://github.com/germsvel/ex_crap). The affected modules are:

- `OeditusCredo.Crap`
- `OeditusCredo.Crap.Score`
- `OeditusCredo.Crap.Complexity`
- `OeditusCredo.Crap.Coverage`
